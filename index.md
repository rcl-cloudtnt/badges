# Introduction

This website provides the criteria for Digital Badges offered by RCL CloudTnT to recognize the skills of DevOps and Software Developers.

## What is a Digital Badge

A digital badge is a .PNG image file with embedded data outlining the skills that were achieved. These badges can be stored in backpacks and displayed by the recipients across various websites.

## How do I earn a badge

You can earn a badge by demonstrating your skill and knowledge in a particular area. This demonstration can include portfolios of your completed projects, contribution to GitHub projects and online skill interviews. Once you provide sufficient evidence for your skill and assessor will award you with a badge.

## Criteria for badges

There is a criteria for earning a digital badge. The criteria will specify the following :
- name of the badge 
- the skills and knowledge that must be demonstrated
- the evidence you must produce
- how you will be assessed
